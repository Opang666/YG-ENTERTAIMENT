About YG

YG Entertainment is a South Korean entertainment company founded in 1996 in Seoul, South Korea. "YG" is an abbreviation of Yang Gun which is taken from the nickname of the founder and executive producer, Yang Hyun-suk.

Welcome to YG, where our music embodies the essence of originality that defines us. At YG, we make no compromises on the perfection of our creations. Fans worldwide recognize the uniqueness of our music and artists, grounded in hip-hop, as a distinctive genre in its own right.
